【诗文】Missing and missing

· 2017-03-31 · # 诗文

发表于 文艺药丸（原文艺词典）

I was poisoned at our meeting
the first time I saw you smiling
but you then were missing
so I know I'm never going to be healing
not even when I'm dreaming

depressed,depressed
I decided to change the state
I tried to find you and I did
I tried to connect you but I cannot
I tried to know you and I did
I tried to forget you but I cannot
maybe that's just it

I'm not really happy
I'm not really unhappy
maybe
I should have step ahead forwardly
though I have no clue how it could be
then it occur to me
that nothing happened between you and me
so I stopped being silly
so I start to take it peacefully
so I'll keep your smile in my memory

still, for that memory
I won't stop visiting
without disturbing
beyond your knowing
